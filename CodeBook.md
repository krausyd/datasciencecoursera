#Tidy.txt

This file contains the mean and std deviation of several information gotten from 30 individuals about activities done (WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING)  while wearing a smartphone (Samsung Galaxy S II) on the waist.  

## Identifiers

The identifiers of the data are:
* **subject** - The ID of the test subject
* **activity** - The type of activity performed when the corresponding measurements were taken. Values can be
* _WALKING_ (value 1)
* _WALKING_UPSTAIRS_ (value 2)
* _WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS_ (value 3)
* _SITTING_ (value 4)
* _STANDING_ (value 5)
* _LAYING_ (value 6)
